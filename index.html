Hello!

Welcome to D.N. Tests! Here you'll find English tests for different levels. Each test checks your grammar, words you know, reading, listening, writing, and speaking. The computer marks most of it straight away. For writing and speaking, your teacher will help.

Don't know which test to pick? Start with A1 — it's the easiest. If it feels too simple, try A2 next time.

What do the levels mean?

These letters and numbers might look funny, but they're just a way to say how much English you know:

A1 I'm starting out
A2 I know some English
B1 I can chat OK
B2 I speak quite well
C1 I'm really good

Tips for taking the test

  • Read each question slowly — there's no timer
  • For the listening parts, you can play the audio more than once
  • Check your spelling — the computer looks for exact words
  • If you don't know an answer, have a guess — it's good practice
  • Ask your teacher if you get stuck
